Privacy Policy for Bindicator Lite

Last updated: 5 July 2026

Bindicator Lite is a home-screen widget app that reminds you which colour of bin to put out for collection.

Data Collection

Bindicator Lite does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The app has no internet access permission and cannot send data anywhere, even if it wanted to.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores your chosen settings — bin collection day and time, colour choices, and whether you use one or two bins per cycle — locally on your device using Android's standard app preferences storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to the developer or any third party.

If your device has Android's automatic backup feature enabled, these settings may be included in your device's standard encrypted backup to your own Google account, in the same way as any other app's settings. This is a standard Android OS feature, not something Bindicator Lite controls or that sends data to the developer.

Permissions Used

  • Notifications (POST_NOTIFICATIONS) — used to show a local reminder notification before your bin collection day changes over. No notification content is sent to or from a server.
  • Receive boot completed (RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED) — used to re-schedule your reminders after your device restarts.

Third-Party Services

Bindicator Lite does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

Bindicator Lite does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children, because it does not collect data at all.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, an updated version will be posted at this same location with a revised "last updated" date.
