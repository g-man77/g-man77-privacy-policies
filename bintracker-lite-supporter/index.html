Privacy Policy for BinTracker Supporter

Last updated: 8 July 2026

BinTracker Supporter is the paid edition of BinTracker Lite, a home-screen widget and companion app that shows which bin colour(s) to put out for collection. It has identical functionality to the free version.

Data Collection

BinTracker Supporter does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The app has no internet access permission and cannot send data anywhere, even if it wanted to.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores your chosen settings — bin collection day and time, and the colour(s) assigned to each collection cycle — locally on your device using Android's standard app preferences storage and a local on-device database. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to the developer or any third party.

If your device has Android's automatic backup feature enabled, these settings may be included in your device's standard encrypted backup to your own Google account, in the same way as any other app's settings. This is a standard Android OS feature, not something BinTracker Supporter controls or that sends data to the developer.

Backup and Restore

BinTracker Supporter includes the same optional "Backup" feature as BinTracker Lite. If you previously used the free version, you can copy your bin colours and collection schedule over manually: open BinTracker Lite and tap Backup → Export (this saves a plain text file to your device's Downloads folder), then open BinTracker Supporter and tap Backup → Import to read that file back in via the standard Android file picker. This transfer happens entirely via a file on your own device — nothing is sent to the developer, to Google, or to any third party.

Permissions Used

  • Receive boot completed (RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED) — used to re-schedule the widget's colour updates after your device restarts.

Third-Party Services

BinTracker Supporter does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. Payment processing for your purchase of this app is handled entirely by Google Play; the developer never sees your payment details.

Children's Privacy

BinTracker Supporter does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children, because it does not collect data at all.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, an updated version will be posted at this same location with a revised "last updated" date.
