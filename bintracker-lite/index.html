Privacy Policy for BinTracker Lite

Last updated: 7 July 2026

BinTracker Lite is a home-screen widget and companion app that shows which bin colour(s) to put out for collection.

Data Collection

BinTracker Lite does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The app has no internet access permission and cannot send data anywhere, even if it wanted to.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores your chosen settings — bin collection day and time, and the colour(s) assigned to each collection cycle — locally on your device using Android's standard app preferences storage and a local on-device database. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to the developer or any third party.

If your device has Android's automatic backup feature enabled, these settings may be included in your device's standard encrypted backup to your own Google account, in the same way as any other app's settings. This is a standard Android OS feature, not something BinTracker Lite controls or that sends data to the developer.

One-Time Import by BinTracker Supporter

BinTracker Lite exposes a read-only, permission-protected interface that only an app signed with the developer's own key can access. This exists solely so that BinTracker Supporter (the paid edition of this app) can offer to copy your bin colours and collection schedule over automatically if you choose to upgrade and still have this free app installed. No other app can read this data, and nothing is sent anywhere off your device.

Permissions Used

  • Receive boot completed (RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED) — used to re-schedule the widget's colour updates after your device restarts.

Third-Party Services

BinTracker Lite does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

BinTracker Lite does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children, because it does not collect data at all.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, an updated version will be posted at this same location with a revised "last updated" date.
