Privacy Policy for CarAdminTracker Lite

Last updated: 8 July 2026

CarAdminTracker Lite is an app and companion home-screen widget that helps you keep track of each vehicle's MOT, road tax, insurance, and any other reminders.

Data Collection

CarAdminTracker Lite does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The app has no internet access permission and cannot send data anywhere, even if it wanted to.

Data Stored on Your Device

The vehicles, dates, and reminders you enter are stored locally on your device using a local on-device database. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to the developer or any third party.

If your device has Android's automatic backup feature enabled, this data may be included in your device's standard encrypted backup to your own Google account, in the same way as any other app's data. This is a standard Android OS feature, not something CarAdminTracker Lite controls or that sends data to the developer.

One-Time Import by CarAdminTracker Supporter

CarAdminTracker Lite exposes a read-only, permission-protected interface that only an app signed with the developer's own key can access. This exists solely so that CarAdminTracker Supporter (the paid edition of this app) can offer to copy your vehicles and their reminders over automatically if you choose to upgrade and still have this free app installed. No other app can read this data, and nothing is sent anywhere off your device.

Permissions Used

  • Receive boot completed (RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED) — used to re-schedule the widget's periodic refresh after your device restarts.

Third-Party Services

CarAdminTracker Lite does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Disclaimer

CarAdminTracker Lite is a reminder aid only. It is your responsibility to keep your vehicle's MOT, tax, and insurance valid — always confirm official renewal dates directly with the DVSA, DVLA, or your insurer.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, an updated version will be posted at this same location with a revised "last updated" date.
