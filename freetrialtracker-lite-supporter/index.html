Privacy Policy for FreeTrialTracker Supporter

Last updated: 7 July 2026

FreeTrialTracker Supporter is the paid edition of FreeTrialTracker Lite, an app and companion home-screen widget that helps you keep track of free trial subscriptions before they expire. It has identical functionality to the free version.

Data Collection

FreeTrialTracker Supporter does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The app has no internet access permission and cannot send data anywhere, even if it wanted to.

Data Stored on Your Device

The subscription names and expiry dates you enter are stored locally on your device using a local on-device database. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to the developer or any third party.

If your device has Android's automatic backup feature enabled, this data may be included in your device's standard encrypted backup to your own Google account, in the same way as any other app's data. This is a standard Android OS feature, not something FreeTrialTracker Supporter controls or that sends data to the developer.

One-Time Import from FreeTrialTracker Lite

If you previously used the free version of this app (FreeTrialTracker Lite) and it is still installed on your device when you first open FreeTrialTracker Supporter, the app will automatically copy your existing trial entries over on first launch, so you don't have to re-enter them. This happens entirely on your own device, directly between the two apps — no data is sent to the developer, to Google, or to any third party. This only happens once, and only if the free app happens to still be installed.

Permissions Used

  • Receive boot completed (RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED) — used to re-schedule the widget's periodic refresh after your device restarts.

Third-Party Services

FreeTrialTracker Supporter does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. Payment processing for your purchase of this app is handled entirely by Google Play; the developer never sees your payment details.

Children's Privacy

FreeTrialTracker Supporter does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children, because it does not collect data at all.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, an updated version will be posted at this same location with a revised "last updated" date.
