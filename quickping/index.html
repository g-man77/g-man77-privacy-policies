Privacy Policy for QuickPing

Last updated: 8 July 2026

QuickPing is a home-screen widget that lets you start a quick countdown timer (1, 5, 10, 15, 20, or 30 minutes) with a single tap, without opening an app.

Data Collection

QuickPing does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The app has no internet access permission and cannot send data anywhere, even if it wanted to.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores which timer (if any) is currently running and when it's due to finish, locally on your device using Android's standard app preferences storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to the developer or any third party.

Permissions Used

  • Wake lock (WAKE_LOCK) — used to keep the device awake for a brief moment so the timer's sound can finish playing, even if the screen is off.
  • Exact alarms (USE_EXACT_ALARM) — used to fire the countdown at precisely the right time, since QuickPing's core function is timing.
  • Receive boot completed (RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED) — used to re-arm or clear an in-progress countdown if your device restarts mid-timer.

Third-Party Services

QuickPing does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

QuickPing does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children, because it does not collect data at all.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, an updated version will be posted at this same location with a revised "last updated" date.
