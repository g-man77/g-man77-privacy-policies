Privacy Policy for SchoolTracker Supporter

Last updated: 8 July 2026 (v1.1)

SchoolTracker Supporter is the paid edition of SchoolTracker Lite, an app and companion home-screen widget that helps parents keep track of each child's weekly PE kit day and any other reminders. It has identical functionality to the free version.

Data Collection

SchoolTracker Supporter does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The app has no internet access permission and cannot send data anywhere, even if it wanted to.

Data Stored on Your Device

The children, PE kit days, and reminders you enter are stored locally on your device using a local on-device database. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to the developer or any third party.

If your device has Android's automatic backup feature enabled, this data may be included in your device's standard encrypted backup to your own Google account, in the same way as any other app's data. This is a standard Android OS feature, not something SchoolTracker Supporter controls or that sends data to the developer.

Backup (Export/Import)

SchoolTracker Supporter has a Backup feature you can use at any time: Export saves your children and reminders as a file in your device's Downloads folder, and Import reads a file you choose back in. If you previously used SchoolTracker Lite, you can export your data there and import it here to bring it across — this is entirely manual and user-initiated, happens directly on your own device, and nothing is sent to the developer, to Google, or to any third party.

Permissions Used

  • Receive boot completed (RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED) — used to re-schedule the widget's periodic refresh after your device restarts.

Third-Party Services

SchoolTracker Supporter does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. Payment processing for your purchase of this app is handled entirely by Google Play; the developer never sees your payment details.

Children's Privacy

SchoolTracker Supporter does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children, because it does not collect data at all. Any information about a user's own children (names, PE kit days, reminders) is entered voluntarily by the parent/guardian using the app and stays on that person's own device.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, an updated version will be posted at this same location with a revised "last updated" date.
