Privacy Policy for SchoolTracker Lite

Last updated: 8 July 2026

SchoolTracker Lite is an app and companion home-screen widget that helps parents keep track of each child's weekly PE kit day and any other reminders.

Data Collection

SchoolTracker Lite does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The app has no internet access permission and cannot send data anywhere, even if it wanted to.

Data Stored on Your Device

The children, PE kit days, and reminders you enter are stored locally on your device using a local on-device database. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to the developer or any third party.

If your device has Android's automatic backup feature enabled, this data may be included in your device's standard encrypted backup to your own Google account, in the same way as any other app's data. This is a standard Android OS feature, not something SchoolTracker Lite controls or that sends data to the developer.

One-Time Import by SchoolTracker Supporter

SchoolTracker Lite exposes a read-only, permission-protected interface that only an app signed with the developer's own key can access. This exists solely so that SchoolTracker Supporter (the paid edition of this app) can offer to copy your children and their reminders over automatically if you choose to upgrade and still have this free app installed. No other app can read this data, and nothing is sent anywhere off your device.

Permissions Used

  • Receive boot completed (RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED) — used to re-schedule the widget's periodic refresh after your device restarts.

Third-Party Services

SchoolTracker Lite does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

SchoolTracker Lite does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children, because it does not collect data at all. Any information about a user's own children (names, PE kit days, reminders) is entered voluntarily by the parent/guardian using the app and stays on that person's own device.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, an updated version will be posted at this same location with a revised "last updated" date.
